ACM CHAPTER INAUGURATION

     The Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science ACM Student Chapter was inaugurated by Shri.Janardhanan P S, Technical Consultant, SunTec Business Solutions,on 26th March, 2015.

     The website of the ACM Chapter was launched on the occasion.During his inaugural address Shri.Janardhanan P S gave his deliberations on the current aspects of computing. He talked about the significance of professional associations like ACM in the College and also congratulated the Department on its initiatives in establishing such student Chapter. He advised the students to use these forums for updating their academic and professional skills.

ACM ANNUAL EVENT 2015

     The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society for the Computer Science/IT community. ACM India was launched in 2010 to increase the focus on the country. ACM India has been organizing annual flagship events to discuss trends in science and technology, and to celebrate ACM's spirit and India's accomplishments in computing.This year the ACM 2015 Annual event was hosted at Birla Institute of Technology and Science Pilani, Goa Campus.


     Arjun RC ,Vivek S and Nikhil Baby of the Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science ACM student chapter attended the event.
