Innovate

Innovate is an initiative of the Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science ACM Student Chapter in association with the Computer Science Association, Clique. The event expects 50 teams from the college. Participants will have 36 hours to build working technology and to create solutions pertaining to college management.Participants will create concepts for software or hardware based program/applications in the following categories:

  • Academics
  • Management
  • Or can be some field that you believe would improve the college management.

Solutions

Participants will create software and hardware applications that provide practical solutions in one of the following areas.



  • Academics
  • Management

Create a solution that would make the academic activities easier to the students.Some sample projects are given below:

  • An online portal that allows teachers to give assignments and in which students can submit their work.
  • An application that allows the students to access the study materials and books uploaded by the professors.

Create hardware or software solution that could actually improve the various aspects of college management like attendance, leave, library etc..Some sample projects in this regard are:

  • A device that would allow teachers to submit the attendance to office directly from the class.
  • A device that could locate a particular student in the college.
  • An app that can mange the library details.
  • A portal that allows student to submit leave and faculty to approve the same.
