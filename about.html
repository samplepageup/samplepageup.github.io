Our Chapter

     ACM, the worlds largest educational and scientific computing society, delivers resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources.

    We are the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM), Student Chapter of Muthoot Institute of Technology and Science, Varikoli. The student chapter was established in september 2014 .We aim to instill the spirit of technology and computing among the students, through the various fields modern Information Technology has developed in.
